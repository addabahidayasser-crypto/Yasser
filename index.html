🏊 Swimming Pro Guide

Master every swimming style

🏊 Freestyle

Tips:

  • Keep body straight and horizontal
  • Rotate your body slightly
  • Kick continuously

🏊 Backstroke

Tips:

  • Keep hips high
  • Look straight up
  • Use steady kicks

🏊 Breaststroke

Tips:

  • Glide after each stroke
  • Keep movements smooth
  • Coordinate arms and legs

🏊 Butterfly

Tips:

  • Use wave-like body motion
  • Kick twice per stroke
  • Stay relaxed
