🌊 Swimming Overview

Swimming is a complete sport that improves endurance, strength, and coordination. A good position in the water is essential to move efficiently and avoid fatigue.

⚙️ Position Tips (Technique)

  • Keep your body horizontal to reduce resistance
  • Align your head with your body
  • Look down, not forward
  • Keep your hips close to the surface
  • Stretch your body forward for better glide

💨 Breathing Tips

  • Exhale slowly underwater
  • Do not hold your breath
  • Keep a regular breathing rhythm
  • Stay relaxed
