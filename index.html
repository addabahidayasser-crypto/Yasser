Swimming

The Art of Moving Through Water

🏊‍♂️ What is Swimming?

Swimming is a water-based activity that involves moving the body through water using arms and legs. It is both a sport and a recreational activity enjoyed worldwide.

🌊 Benefits of Swimming

  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Builds muscle strength
  • Low impact on joints
  • Reduces stress and improves mood

🏅 Types of Swimming Strokes

  • Freestyle
  • Backstroke
  • Breaststroke
  • Butterfly

💡 Fun Fact

Swimming is one of the few sports that works almost every muscle in the body at the same time!
